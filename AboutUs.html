About Our Firm

Sohail Manga Attorneys was founded in Lenasia by Director Sohail Manga, who graduated from the prestigious University of the Witwatersrand with an LLB degree. With a vision to provide accessible and professional legal services to the community, our firm began its journey with a commitment to excellence and integrity.

Over the years, our firm has successfully completed numerous cases across various areas of law. We have achieved significant victories in criminal cases, divorce matters, property transfers, estate planning, labor disputes, and civil litigation. Our track record of success is built on meticulous preparation, strategic thinking, and unwavering dedication to our clients' interests.

From our humble beginnings in Lenasia, we have expanded to serve clients across Johannesburg with two strategic branches in Lenasia and Robertsham.

At Sohail Manga Attorneys, we strive to give our clients the best legal representation possible. We understand that each client's situation is unique, and we approach every case with personalized attention and care. Our team of experienced attorneys works collaboratively to ensure comprehensive legal solutions for even the most complex matters.

We have handled numerous cases for diverse clients, from individuals facing personal legal challenges to businesses navigating complex commercial matters. Our commitment to justice, combined with our deep understanding of South African law, has established us as a trusted legal partner in the community.

Community Involvement

Community Service

Partnering with Gift of the Givers Foundation

During the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, our Director Sohail Manga demonstrated his commitment to community service by providing pro bono legal assistance to the Gift of the Givers Foundation.

He assisted with all legal aspects of establishing and operating their COVID-19 treatment facility, ensuring that the foundation could operate efficiently and effectively in their humanitarian mission.

This included navigating complex regulatory requirements, drafting necessary agreements, and providing ongoing legal counsel to support their critical pandemic response efforts.

"It was an honor to support Gift of the Givers in their vital work during the pandemic. Legal expertise should serve not only our clients but also our community in times of need."
