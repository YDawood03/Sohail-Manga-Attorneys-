Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling either of our offices, sending an email, or visiting us in person during business hours. We offer both in-person and virtual consultations for your convenience.

What should I bring to my first consultation?

Please bring any relevant documents related to your case, including identification, court documents, contracts, correspondence, police reports, medical records, and any other materials that might help us understand your situation.

What are your consultation fees?

We offer an initial consultation to discuss your legal matter and provide an estimate of costs. Fees vary depending on the complexity of your case and the services required. During your consultation, we will provide transparent information about our fee structure.

Do you offer after-hours appointments?

Yes, we offer after-hours appointments by special arrangement for clients who cannot visit during regular business hours. Please contact us in advance to schedule an after-hours consultation.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. All communications with our firm are protected by attorney-client privilege and kept strictly confidential in accordance with legal ethics and data protection laws. Your privacy and confidentiality are our top priorities.

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please call us directly for immediate attention. Emergency situations are handled with priority.
